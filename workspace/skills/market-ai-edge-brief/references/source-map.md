# Source map

Use direct, high-signal sources first.

## AI and engineering
- OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind, Meta AI, Microsoft, NVIDIA
- Hugging Face, GitHub, major engineering blogs
- MIT Technology Review for synthesis
- Hacker News for discovery, not authority

## Markets, banks, and macro technology
- Financial Times
- Bloomberg
- Reuters
- Barron's
- The Economist
- Bank and exchange newsrooms when primary confirmation matters

## Blockchain and digital assets
Use selectively and only when strategically relevant:
- CoinDesk
- Cointelegraph only as a discovery layer, not primary authority
- Major exchange or protocol blogs when primary facts matter
- ETF, custody, regulation, or market-structure developments matter more than token chatter

## Filtering rules
- Prefer primary documents, company announcements, and high-quality financial reporting.
- Use web search to fill gaps, not to drive the whole brief.
- If a source is sensational but not decision-useful, drop it.
- If multiple outlets repeat the same fact, cite the one with the best direct evidence.
