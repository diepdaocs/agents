# Target companies

Use this ladder when expanding beyond the bundled script.

## 1. Banks and bank-adjacent employers
Force-check these first for Singapore or APAC engineering roles:
- JPMorgan
- Citi
- DBS
- Goldman Sachs
- Standard Chartered
- Morgan Stanley
- Bank of America
- UBS

Prioritize technology, electronic trading, risk, market data, platform, cloud, SRE-for-trading, and data-platform roles.

## 2. Trading firms and hedge funds
Force-check these weekly when the user wants the best finance-engineering opportunities:
- Jane Street
- Jump Trading
- IMC
- Flow Traders
- Virtu
- Maven
- Point72
- Hudson River Trading
- DRW

Prioritize software engineering, quant platform, infra, low-latency, data infra, exchange connectivity, and developer productivity roles.

## 3. Exchanges and market infrastructure
Check when banking/trading supply is thin or when the user wants broader market-tech coverage:
- Nasdaq
- CME Group
- ICE
- LSEG
- Bloomberg
- SGX
- Broadridge
- FIS / Fidelity National Information Services

## 4. Selective digital-assets employers
Use selectively, after traditional finance and trading firms:
- Coinbase
- OKX
- Kraken
- BitGo
- FalconX

Keep the shortlist diversified. Crypto-native employers should not dominate unless the user asks.

## Search heuristics
- Prefer direct hosted job pages or ATS result pages that expose individual role URLs.
- Use LinkedIn or web search only to discover the direct employer URL.
- If the job page is opaque, note that confidence is lower instead of pretending certainty.
- For Singapore compensation calibration, cross-check with MyCareersFuture or local market norms when possible.
